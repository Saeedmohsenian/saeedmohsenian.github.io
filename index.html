About Me

I am a Mechanical and Bioengineering professional driven by a passion for using high-fidelity simulation to solve complex, real-world problems. My journey began with a deep dive into thermal-fluid sciences, optimizing automotive cooling systems with CFD. This practical, hardware-focused experience taught me how to bridge the gap between a digital model and a physical product.

Seeking a new challenge, I pivoted to the biomedical field, applying the same core engineering principles to model the intricate fluid dynamics of the human body. As a researcher and at Biogen, I developed sophisticated CFD models for drug delivery and CSF flow, leading to multiple first-author publications. Today, I am eager to apply my unique, cross-disciplinary experience to innovative engineering challenges in thermal management, energy, or advanced transportation.
