About Me

My engineering career has been a journey from the macroscopic to the microscopic, driven by a fascination with the fundamental principles of thermal-fluid sciences. I began by tackling real-world hardware challenges in the automotive industry, using CFD to design and optimize high-performance cooling systems. This experience taught me how to bridge the gap between a digital simulation and a physical, working product.

Seeking to apply these principles to a more intricate and impactful domain, I pivoted to bioengineering. At Biogen and in my academic research, I modeled the most complex machine I've ever encountered: the human body. This work, which led to multiple first-author publications, solidified my expertise in multiphysics modeling and fluid-structure interaction. Today, I am passionate about using this unique, cross-disciplinary background to create innovative solutions for the next generation of technology.

Research Areas

Mechanical Engineering / Heat Transfer / Fluid Dynamics

  • Multiphase Flow Modeling: Gas-liquid and liquid-solid transport; Nanofluid dynamics using Eulerian/Lagrangian models.
  • Thermal Management & Heat Transfer: Passive and active thermal systems for electronics; Microscale and nanoscale heat transfer.
  • Vortex Dynamics & Transient Fluid Mechanics: Unsteady, high-energy-density flow phenomena; Pulsatile or oscillatory flows for mixing.
  • CFD & Optimization: Turbulence modeling (RANS/LES), non-Newtonian flow; Design optimization using metaheuristics.

Biomedical Engineering / Biotransport / Soft Tissue Mechanics

  • CSF Hydrodynamics and Brain Biomechanics: Simulation of CSF flow; Brain-tissue deformation and intracranial pressure dynamics.
  • Image-Based Modeling: 3D reconstruction from MRI data for personalized FSI and transport simulations.
  • Soft Tissue Biomechanics: Modeling of deformation, viscoelasticity, and mechanical stress in soft tissues.
  • Fluid-Structure Interaction (FSI): Coupling between flow and tissue deformation in cardiovascular or central nervous systems.
